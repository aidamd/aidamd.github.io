About

I am a third-year Computer Science PhD student in University of Southern California (USC) working with Morteza Dehghani.
My main research interest includes designing and developing Natural Language Processing models to answer social science question. More recently, I have become interested in understanding human biases and their effects on designing machine learning models. I a member and research assistant in Computational Social Science Lab, where I have the opportunity to collaborate with researchers from social pscychology and computer science.
